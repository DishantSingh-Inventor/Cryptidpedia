Chupacabra

The Chupacabra, translating to "goat-sucker"(lol sad to be him, it sucks) in Spanish. This is a legendary cryptid reported in parts of the America. The first sighting occurred in Puerto Rico in 1995, where it was described as a bipedal, reptilian creature with spikes along its spine, large red eyes and they prey on livestocks and suck them dry for there blood.

Later reports in the southern United States described a completely different creature: a hairless canine-like beast walking on all four legs. Biologists who studied carcasses of these Texas "chupacabras" confirmed them to be coyotes or wild dogs suffering from severe cases of sarcoptic mange.

  • Eye-witnesses describe bizarre leaping capabilities
