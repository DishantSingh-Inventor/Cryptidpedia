Mothman

Mothman is a legendary winged humanoid creature sighted in the Point Pleasant area of West Virginia from November 15, 1966, to December 15, 1967. Eyewitnesses described the entity as a man-sized creature with large wings and glowing, reflective red eyes that were set near the top of its shoulders.

The sightings ended abruptly following the tragic collapse of the Silver Bridge on December 15, 1967, which resulted in the deaths of 46 people. This led to a popular belief that Mothman was either an omen, a herald of disaster, or a supernatural warning system trying to alert the town.

Evidence

  • Over 100 independent eyewitness reports over a single year
  • Coincidence with actual structure the bridge collapse.
  • Corroborating reports of static interference and UFOs

Flaws

  • High chances of misidentification of Sandhill Cranes
  • No photographic or physical evidence recovered
  • Too much hyped by the local media sensations
